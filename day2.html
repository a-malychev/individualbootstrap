Day 2: Cardio and Ab Options

Workout Option 1

This is the basic cardio option. Choose just one of the following and pair with the ab workout linked below

  • 20-30 minute moderate paced run
  • 30 minute eliptical
  • 30 minute bike
  • 60 minute walk
Ab Workout 1
Workout Option 2

This workout hits cardio intensity a little harder. Again, choose one and pair with the ab workout linked. Pair your cardio with a half mile warm up jog and a half mile cool down.

  • Hill sprints (about 20 minutes)
  • 4 x 800 meter challenging pace intervals
  • 2 x 1600 meter challenging pace intervals
  • 8 x 100 meter dashes
Ab Workout 2
Workout Option 3

Options to change it up from typical cardio.

  • Play a basketball Game
  • Swim laps for half an hour
  • Do intervals on the stairmaster
  • Hike for an hour
Ab Workout 3

Tips

  • Have fun with this day
  • Once your body adapts to bodyweight ab exercises, add weights!
