Day 4: Upper Body

Workout Option 1

This workout focuses on the triceps and chest.

  • Chest Press Machine 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdown Machine 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Chest fly Machine 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Tricep Dips 3 sets x 12 reps
Get More Targeted Arm Workouts
Workout Option 2

In this option we will challenge the back,shoulders, and the biceps.

  • Bicep Curls 3 sets x 10 each arm
  • Rows 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Pull Down Bar 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Overhead Arm Raises 3 sets x 12 reps
Workout Option 3

This options is the at home and dumbell version that works all upper body muscles.

  • Lateral Arm Raises 3 sets x 20 alternating arms
  • Pushups 3 sets x 20
  • Pullups 3 sets x however many you can do
  • Bicep Curls 3 sets x 10 each arm

Tips

  • Warm up for arm day by running, using the elliptical, or doing arm circles
  • You can mix these options and just do one all around upper body day
